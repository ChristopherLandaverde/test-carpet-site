Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a consultation take?

Initial consultations typically take 1-2 hours, depending on the complexity of your project. We take the time to understand your vision and provide personalized recommendations.

Do you offer in-home consultations?

Yes, we offer in-home consultations for residential projects. Our family members will visit your space to take measurements and discuss design options in your environment.

What information should I prepare?

It's helpful to have room dimensions, inspiration photos, budget range, and timeline in mind. But don't worry—we'll guide you through the entire process.

How long does custom carpet production take?

Custom carpet production typically takes 8-12 weeks, depending on complexity and size. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services throughout Southern California. Our team ensures perfect installation and provides care instructions.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater Los Angeles area, Orange County, and select areas of Southern California. For projects outside our service area, we can arrange shipping.
