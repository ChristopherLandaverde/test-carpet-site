Caring for Your Investment

Our carpets are designed to last generations with proper care. Here's how to maintain their beauty and value.

Regular Maintenance

  • Vacuum weekly with a brushless attachment
  • Rotate carpets every 6 months
  • Use rug pads for protection
  • Avoid direct sunlight exposure

Professional Cleaning

  • Professional cleaning every 2-3 years
  • Use certified carpet cleaning services
  • Test cleaning products on hidden areas
  • Allow proper drying time

Storage & Protection

  • Store in climate-controlled environments
  • Roll, don't fold for storage
  • Use acid-free paper for wrapping
  • Protect from pests and moisture
