Our Story

Three generations of passion, tradition, and unwavering commitment to crafting the world's finest handwoven carpets.

Family Heritage Since 1987

A Legacy of Excellence

In 1987, Antonio and Maria Rodriguez opened a small carpet workshop in Beverly Hills with a simple mission: to create handcrafted carpets that would become family heirlooms. What began as a modest family business has grown into one of the most respected names in luxury carpet craftsmanship.

🏠

1987 - The Beginning

Antonio and Maria Rodriguez establish their first workshop, bringing traditional Persian weaving techniques to Beverly Hills. Their first commission: a custom carpet for the Beverly Hills Hotel.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

1995 - Family Grows

Their son Carlos joins the business, bringing modern design sensibilities while preserving traditional craftsmanship. The family expands their workshop and begins serving high-end residential clients.

🏆

2005 - Recognition

Premium Carpets Co receives the prestigious "Craftsmanship Excellence Award" from the American Society of Interior Designers. Their work is featured in Architectural Digest.

🌍

2015 - Global Reach

Third generation Sofia Rodriguez joins the family business, expanding their reach to international markets while maintaining the same commitment to quality and tradition.
